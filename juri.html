This sadistic thrillseeker enjoys the strife and suffering of others, taking immense joy in obliterating her foes.
Without revenge against M. Bison as a motivator, she whiles away her time in a gloomy haze.
Juri is well suited to explosive, aggressive rushdown and strike/throw mix that's empowered by space control with her
ground normals and storing up stocks to improve her special moves, making her a menace at close and mid range.
She can also get some very massive and flashy combos off of her hits, making her a great pick for someone who
loves pulling off long combo strings.
